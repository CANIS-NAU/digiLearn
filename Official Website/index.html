Our Project: The Digital Backpack

The Digital Backpack system is an app and associated proxy server that will enable remote learning for students without reliable internet access at home. The app, DigiPack, will automatically perform search queries, download or upload homework assignments, and pull documents requested by the user, or suggested by a teacher, whenever Wifi is available. These tasks can be queued offline, and when a connection is available, the app will automatically retrieve the requested content. While the user is offline, the proxy server will act on the user's behalf to accomplish the tasks described above.
